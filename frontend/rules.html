How PEPEDAWN Decentralized Lottery Works

Round Lifecycle

  • Round Duration: 2 weeks per round
  • Buy Your Tickets: Round opens for betting, track your stats in real-time
  • Proof Submission: Submit puzzle proof after your first ticket purchase for +40% weight bonus
  • Drawing Winners: Chainlink VRF provides verifiable randomness
  • Prize Distribution: Automatic Emblem Vault NFT distribution to winners
  • Winners Claiming: Winners claim their Emblem Vault prizes using Merkle proofs
  • Winners Claiming: Round Participants Cap: 100

Ticket Office Rules

  • Minimum Purchase: 0.005 ETH (1 ticket)
  • Bundle Discounts:
    • 5 tickets = 0.0225 ETH (10% discount)
    • 10 tickets = 0.04 ETH (20% discount)
  • Per-Wallet Cap: 1.0 ETH maximum per round
  • Network: Ethereum mainnet/testnet only
  • Minimum Distribution: 10 tickets required for prizes to be distributed
  • Refund Policy: If fewer than 10 tickets are sold, all participants receive full refunds

Skill-Weighted Odds

  • Puzzle Proofs: Submit one proof per round
  • Weight Bonus: +40% multiplier on your effective weight
  • Hard Cap: Maximum +40% increase, no stacking
  • Verification: On-chain or signed message verification

Randomness & Fairness

  • VRF Provider: Chainlink VRF v2/v2.5
  • Snapshot: Eligible tickets and weights captured before VRF request
  • Reproducibility: All randomness derived from request ID, seed, and block
  • No Manipulation: No off-chain salts after wagering begins

Prize Distribution

  • Prize Tiers:
    • 1st Place: 1x Fake Pack (3 PEPEDAWN cards in one Emblem Vault)
    • 2nd Place: 1x Kek Pack (2 PEPEDAWN cards in one Emblem Vault)
    • 3rd-10th Place: 1x Pepe Pack each (1 PEPEDAWN card per Emblem Vault)
  • Total Winners: 10 ticket winners per round, 10 prize packs distributed
  • Source: Emblem Vault preloaded assets
  • Distribution: Automatic post-VRF fulfillment
  • Transparency: All winner assignments recorded on-chain
  • Multiple Wins: Same wallet can win multiple prizes in one round

Fee Structure

  • Creators: 80% of fees
  • Next Round Pot: 20% of fees
  • Settlement: Applied at round close
  • Events: All fee distributions logged on-chain

Winning Odds

Your winning odds depend on:

  • Total number of participants
  • Your ticket count and successful proof submission

Atomic Dispenser (Progress Tracking)

  • Visual Tracker: 10 individual ticket icons (üé´) show dispenser progress in real-time
  • Status Message: Displays "minimum 10 tickets required" until threshold is reached
  • Refund Policy: If threshold not met by round end, all participants receive full refunds automatically

Technical Details

  • Smart Contracts: Solidity 0.8.x on Ethereum with OpenZeppelin security modules
  • Testing: 151 Foundry unit tests with 100% functional requirement coverage
  • Frontend: Minimal vanilla JS with ethers v6 for Web3 interaction
  • Performance: Optimized bundle size for fast loading on all devices
  • VRF Provider: Chainlink VRF v2.5 for cryptographically verifiable randomness
  • Data Storage: Winners stored on IPFS, verified on-chain with Merkle roots
  • Leaderboard: Live on-chain participant data with real-time odds calculation
  • Eligibility: Configurable denylist enforcement, pausable for emergencies

Important Disclaimers

  • Minimum Threshold: Rounds with fewer than 10 tickets result in full refunds
  • No Guarantees: Prize distribution only occurs if minimum threshold is met
  • Weighted Lottery: Higher weight increases odds but does not guarantee wins
  • One Proof Attempt: Only one puzzle proof submission allowed per wallet per round
  • Network Only: Ethereum mainnet/testnet only, no L2 networks
